The Power Crystals!

Instructions:

You'll be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you'll add a specific amount of points to your number.

You win by matching your total score to the game number. You lose if your number goes above the game number.

The crystal's point value remains hidden until you click upon it.

Each new game, each crystal's value change.
